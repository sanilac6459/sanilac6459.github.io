SEP11 Freedom Project

For this year-long project, we used Javascript which was the course of SEP11 and a tool we choose to help create our project. So, me and my group decided to make Keysanity which a speed typing game.

For our creation, we used JS and our tool, p5js, to make a fun and interactive game for users to play.

The most challenging part of making our project was deciding what to make. Since we had to start deciding at the beginning of the year, my group and I was unsure how much we need to learn to make our creation since we learned a lot abput Javascript at that time. We also had trouble deciding on a tool to use because when my group and I were tinkering, we followed tutorials and documentations but still found it confusing to process. Personally, I felt like my group and I was trying to create something complex
