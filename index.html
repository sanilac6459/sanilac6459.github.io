About Me

I’m a dedicated Computer Science student at CUNY Hunter College and part of the CS Daedalus Honors Scholar Program. My journey began in high school, where I took a four-year software engineering course. While solving algorithms and developing personal projects, I found my passion for programming and technology. I envisioned myself working in the tech industry, turning my imagination into innovations that shape the future. Finding something I love doing has driven me to pursue a career in software engineering. Since then, I’ve worked on many personal projects, developed expertise in various programming languages, tools, and frameworks, and gained insights into the industry through events, workshops, and internships.

As a woman in tech, I want to pursue a career where I can make an impact. Technology is an ever-growing field, with new ideas and innovations serving as stepping stones to endless possibilities. Seeing how people dedicate their passion and hard work to their ideas has motivated me to push my limits and build the confidence to contribute to future tech milestones.

Despite my commitment to be a software engineer, I’m also open to exploring other tech fields like full stack development, IT, cybersecurity, ML/AI, UI/UX, etc.
